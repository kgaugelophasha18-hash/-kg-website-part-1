Our Story

Ordinary was developed under Deciem, the Toronto based beauty company founded in the year 2013 by Brandon Truaxe, an industry visionary who had previously launched Euoko and indeed Labs. The company was built to defy traditional beauty and ensure that the customer wasn't paying for a long chain of mark-ups . In August 2016 , Deciem launched The Ordinary with an impressive 27 product lineup , including formulas that remain best sellers nearly a decade later.

Our Mission

  • We aim to provide transparancy by offering clear communication about ingridients.

  • Educating Consumers by guiding users into understanding their skin needs rather than being influenced by market trends.

  • Ensuring Sustainability by providing dermatologically tested , high quality and eco -friendly products that aim to deliver real and gentle results.
